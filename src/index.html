Migraine Weather Risk

Migraine Risk (scaled %)

How This Works

This tool analyzes weather factors that research has linked to migraine sensitivity and converts them into a single risk score.

  • Atmospheric pressure changes
  • Humidity levels
  • Temperature extremes
  • Wind speed and intensity

Your risk score updates every time you check the current weather for a location.

How Your Risk Score Is Calculated

  • Humidity: Above 70% adds 20 points, below 30% adds 10 points.
  • Pressure: Below 1005 mb adds 30 points, above 1020 mb adds 15 points.
  • Temperature: Very cold (< 3°C) or hot (> 28°C) adds 25 points.
  • Wind: Strong winds (> 25 kph) add 15 points.
  • Score Range: The score is capped at 100 and grouped as Low, Medium or High risk.

0–39 is Low, 40–69 is Medium, and 70–100 is High migraine weather risk.
